Who is TheOtherAlex?

Alex is a multi-media content creator and an avid gamer. He has a passion for all things entertainment and loves utilizing his creativity to connect with people who share his enthusiasm. He was the creator of Media Drones, Nominated Short Film Director/Writer at "180 Film Festival", created a Let's Play Channel and is currently working to be a Game Designer. He received an associates degree in Cinema and a certificate for Front-End Developer.
